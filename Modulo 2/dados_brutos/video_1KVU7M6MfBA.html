#reworkDL
At the Deep Learning Summit in Montreal, for the first time ever, RE•WORK brought together the ‘Godfathers of AI’ to appear not only at the same event, but on a joint panel discussion. We saw Yoshua Bengio, Yann LeCun and Geoffrey Hinton come together to share their most cutting edge research progressions as well as discussing the landscape of AI and the deep learning ecosystem in Canada. This panel discussion is moderated by Joelle Pineau, Associate Professor of Computer Science at McGill University.

In this Data Science interview prep video, Jaidev Deshpande, Senior Data Scientist at Gramener and Mentor at Springboard, discusses the most asked Data Science interview questions and the best ways to answer them. Data Scientists are in high demand today and cracking a Data Science interview could be your first step towards cracking open a rewarding and lucrative Data Science career. This video is suitable for technology professionals just starting up with Data Science tutorials and also for those who are preparing for Data Science jobs.

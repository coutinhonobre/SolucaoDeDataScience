#IAMRISING
Here's a video dedicated to women in #ArtificialIntelligence, who have excelled in STEM and broken the barriers to achieving new heights.

The World Economic Forum’s Global Gender Gap Report 2020 found that there is still a long way to go to achieve worldwide gender parity. It will still take us 99.5 years to close the gender gap. While we are seeing a change in society is it enough?

Women have proven that they excel in the board room as good as they do in bringing up their kids, but why do we still need to fight for equal pay?

Organizations today are working towards gender inclusion, but why is it just limited to short programs designed around women's day?

While there are many questions to be answered, We are Rising step by step, fighting for the equality that we deserve.
For more details, visit: https://rising.analyticsindiasummit.com/

20thMarch 2020 | Bangalore

In the biggest meeting of women Data Science leaders from across the domain, women professionals from the industry and academia will come together for The Rising to be held in Hotel Radisson Blu, on March 20, 2020. The conference will serve as a forum for exchange for building a better idea for women participating in STEM and will also highlight the achievements and career interests of women in data science.
The conference will provide a platform to leading women visionaries to dive into the field of data science and share their perspective on how to build a career in this buzzing field and their role in data science. Through a series of talks and informal sessions, this one-day conference will empower women and help them in the development of leadership skills.

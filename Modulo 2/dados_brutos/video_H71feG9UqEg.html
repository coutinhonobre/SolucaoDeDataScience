Feb Candle Light Artificial Intelligence AI Machine Learning Stock Trading System Forward Testing
"Candle Light" is an Artificial Intelligence Data Wave Processing System: This computer program applying AI (Machine Learning), statistics and pattern recognition technologies to dig, analyze and process data oscillation, a game-changing trading mode in stocks, options and FOREX markets...

The “Candle Light” Machine Learning Trading System applies unique analytical algorithms to artiﬁcial intelligence (AI) technologies, to build predictive investment models on dozens of stocks. This innovative Automated data driven investment process can remove signiﬁcant human bias and errors.

This system scan around 3,000~5,000 groups of data, 24 x 7, to seek repeatable features, calculate their odds, then test these features with real forward market data. If they can pass these forward tests and generate some decent profit with a high odd, then we will adopt this signal to execute proper actions.

"If you invent a breakthrough in artificial intelligence, so machines can learn, that is worth 10 Microsofts." — Bill Gates (2004)

Talk is cheap, let's practice and test it. After every trading day close, we will publish signals and actions generated by “Candle Light” Machine Learning Trading System for USA stock markets. A 24 Hours delayed report will be published on social medias for free. Another in-advance signals and actions plan and daily report is open to a private observer group members only, will be sent them before next trading day. These members can read a daily report and watch how the “Candle Light” system is running, see what we have done on current day and what we will do on next trading day, based on the smart work of Machine Learning Trading System.

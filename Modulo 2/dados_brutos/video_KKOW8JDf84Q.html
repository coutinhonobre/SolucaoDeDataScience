In this episode of Now Speaking, Rohit Pandharkar, Head of Data Science at Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd. speaks on the challenges faced in data science implementation. Integrating data from multiple sources is a challenge for all companies across sectors. Mahindra tackles this by pouring multiple siloed data into several data marts for different Mahindra companies and their respective databases. This requires data scientists and data engineers with the right set of skills who can gauge data quality and leverage it to solve business problems, gain insights and eventually show positive business impact.

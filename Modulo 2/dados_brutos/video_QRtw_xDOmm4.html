Who here wants to learn data science? GOJEK Data Scientists Ridlo and Dipta will give you a quick course on how we solve a problem with a Data Science approach in GOJEK! Because for us, Data Science is not just about making a fancy machine learning models. You have to consider whether or not your model is impactful enough for, at least, people around you. This time, we’re trying to solve a Data Science problem in our newest (and imaginary) product, GO-HOME. We want to create a Data Science model to predict the pricing of the houses that we (pretend to) sell in GO-HOME. See how this Data duo cracks the problem in GO-LEARN

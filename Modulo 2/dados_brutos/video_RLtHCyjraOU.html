Publicado em 22 de nov. de 2019

Growing up watching Indian movies, I always pictured myself living the life of the characters, and sometimes even the actors playing those roles. So, I wanted to make a Celebrity Doppelgänger Finder to see who my celebrity look-alike is! This project was implemented with the help of the Face Recognition API using an SVM model from SciKitLearn. Simply run the script on terminal, input your favorite picture of yourself, and voila, your celebrity doppelgänger is revealed!

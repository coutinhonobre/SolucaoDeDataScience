Learn how to make Radial Bar Chart in Tableau through this amazing tutorial!

Get the dataset and completed Tableau workbook here:
https://www.superdatascience.com/yt-t...

Radial Bar charts are a variation of pie charts which shows the relationship of the part to the whole, but what makes them powerful is the fact that they can contain subcategories. They are great for a time series, but did we mention they actually get the heads turning?

Go with us on this short journey in learning how to create this specific type of bar chart - we even have a surprise at the end on how to improve it even more!

When do we use a radial bar chart?

Consider using a radial bar chart when:

There is a hierarchy in the data – For example, product category and product sub-category
None of the values to be plotted is negative or zero
Not more than 7 categories are present per data series
Categories represent parts of a whole in each ring

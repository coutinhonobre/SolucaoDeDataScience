A short online addendum to the fall 2019 Machine Learning course at UW's Professional Maters Program. The video gives a walk-though of how to approach the in-class Kaggle machine learning competition assignment, including simple steps for tuning hyper parameters, sample code, an easy optimization, interpreting the results of your modeling runs, iterating, making your submission, and achieving success.

You can view the full machine learning lecture series here: https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list...

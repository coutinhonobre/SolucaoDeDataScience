Data Science is a joint bachelor’s program of Tilburg University and Eindhoven University of Technology.

In the English-taught bachelor's program, you learn to analyze the large amounts of data that today's society operates on. You learn to spot trends for businesses, government agencies or nonprofit organizations. Besides the technical side of the program, you will be focusing on societal issues - legal, social, ethical, business, and entrepreneurial - relating to Data Science.

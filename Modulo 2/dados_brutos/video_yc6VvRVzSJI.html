2- Cross Sell - Selling an additional products to an existing customer

3- Personalized Recommendation -
4- Pricing products
5- Loyalty analysis
6-Campaign Analysis
7- Market Basket Analysis
8- A/B testing
9-CTR rate prediction
10-Segmentation Analysis
